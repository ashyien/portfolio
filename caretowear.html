Care to Wear.

Tracks your clothing consumption

Care to Wear is a conceptual interactive prototype that tracks your resource consumption based on the clothing you add to your wardrobe.

Problem

Based on a survey our team had conducted, we received 92 responses about spending habits for clothes and interviewed 4 environmentally conscious people. We discovered that 67% said they were not well educated about fast fashion. More than half of the responses mentioned they get their clothes from fast fashion retailers.

From these insights, it led us to ask ourselves How do we show the direct link between clothing consumption and the environment, as well as provide methods for finding clothes sustainably that do not break the bank.
