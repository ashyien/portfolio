Opposit Type Foundry.

A company that finds, purchases and acquires typefaces to distribute and share to designers.

All the typefaces provided are crafted precisely by designers, who distribute the fonts as files intended for the end user to install on their person computer, which would in turn activate them in their various word-processing applications.

Our Research

For this project, we had to create a webpage of our own .

We discovered that 67% said they were not well educated about fast fashion. More than half of the responses mentioned they get their clothes from fast fashion retailers and are unaware that there are alternate ways of becoming sustainable. From these insights, it led us to ask ourselves how do we show the direct link between clothing consumption and the environment, as well as provide methods for finding clothes sustainably that do not break the bank.

User Experience Issues

We then talked to seven people with knowledge about sustainability in general ranging from very limited to very well informed. Two were brought up in a sustainable household, three learned about environmental sustainability through education, and two gained awareness through friends/peers

Progess Information

User Issues

Users had difficulty understanding what the information provided on this page meant due to the lack of context. They were unaware what kind of information was being provided.

Solution

Our solution was to provide more context and information in settings. Also, allow users to set goals for themselves and find ways for users to meet the “goal” instead of just buying

Inadequate Affordances

User Issues

Users weren’t aware that you could switch categories by clicking the name. One user thought the line was a search bar.

Solution

Our solution was to use visual hints that there are categories and consider the use of side swiping to switch categories

Inputting Text Manually

User Issues

Most users were unaware that the underlined text can be edited and changed when logging in a customized item.

Solution

Our solution was to edit type to make it more obvious that it can be clicked and add instruction to help inform users that the text is editable at this stage
